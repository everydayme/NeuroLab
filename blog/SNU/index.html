The CCS Lab has moved to Seoul National University

01 Sep 2017

The Computational Clinical Science (CCS) Lab (happylaboratory.org) has moved to Seoul National University as of Sep 1, 2017. There are several benefits of becoming inaugural members at SNU including more opportunities for publications and interaction with the PI. Feel free to contact Dr. Ahn for more info (also check here). Come join us and contribute to building a happy and productive lab!
